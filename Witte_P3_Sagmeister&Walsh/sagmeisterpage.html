Stefan Sagmeister's style is a blend of experimental typography, conceptual thinking, playfulness, and a commitment to personal projects. His work stands out for its creativity, innovation, and the ability to engage viewers on both an intellectual and emotional level. He often likes to play around with social taboos in his work, taking inspiration from designer Tibor Kalman, who he worked with for a short period of time. Much of Sagmeister's inspiration comes from Kalman.

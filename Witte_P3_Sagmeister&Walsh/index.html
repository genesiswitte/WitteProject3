Sagmeister & Walsh is a NYC based design firm that comes together through the partnership of Stefan Sagmeister and Jessica Walsh. Together, these two create self-initiated creative art projects. They focus on identities, commercials, websites, apps, films, books, and a wide variety of client work as well as work for audiences. You will often see these two combining a variety of mediums throughout all of their work.
